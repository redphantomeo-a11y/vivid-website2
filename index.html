How does the obstacle detection work?

Is the crutch adjustable?

Yes, SmartStride comes with height adjustment options to fit users between 5'0" and 6'4".

How long does the battery last?

With typical use, the battery lasts approximately 7 days. It charges fully in about 2 hours via USB-C.
